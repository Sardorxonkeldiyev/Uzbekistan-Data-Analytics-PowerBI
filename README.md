# 🏗️ Construction Project Management & Business Intelligence Dashboard

A comprehensive, multi-page Power BI dashboard designed to provide high-level visibility into project statuses, budgets, timelines, resource utilization, and procurement material tracking for large-scale construction developments.

---

## 🇬🇧 ENGLISH VERSION

### 🌍 Project Overview
This enterprise-level Business Intelligence project delivers cross-sectional analytics for construction project management. It empowers stakeholders to monitor budget deviations, track operational costs, manage workforce efficiency, and streamline material procurement across multiple high-value projects (including residential TJM, hotels, and commercial offices).

### 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Used
*   **Power BI Desktop:** Advanced data modeling, star schema architecture, dynamic DAX measures, and corporate UI/UX development.
*   **Power Query (ETL):** Extraction, cleaning, and transforming historical construction and HR operational logs into structured reporting dimensions.
*   **Data Visualization:** Interactive line charts, advanced breakdown matrices, tree maps, donut charts, and dynamic filtering systems.

### 📈 Detailed Dashboard Architecture & Insights

#### 1. Project Overview & Cost Control Panel (`Uzbekistan_data_Eng_3.jpg`)
*   **High-Level Tracking:** Monitors 10 active/planned projects with core KPIs: Budget Deviation ($4\text{bn}$), Actual Costs ($53\text{bn}$), and Total Planned Costs ($57\text{bn}$).
*   **Variance Analysis:** A comprehensive bar-chart comparing *Planned Cost vs. Actual Cost* across specific projects (e.g., Yakkasaroy Hotel, Chilonzor TJM, Olmazor multi-story complexes).
*   **Timeline Monitoring:** Track project durations, start/end dates, responsible lead experts, and dynamic project status distributions (In progress, Planned, Completed).

#### 2. Project Cost & Materials Procurement Procurement (`Uzbekistan_data_Eng_2.jpg`)
*   **Procurement Metrics:** Tracks 10 core material types across $68.70\text{bn}$ total monthly expenses, deeply evaluating $23\text{bn}$ incurred purely on materials.
*   **Cost Segmentation:** Analysis of average costs across standard operational types: Salaries (Ish haqi), Logistics (Transport), Utilities (Kommunal), and raw Materials.
*   **Supply Chain Analytics:** Tracks supplier market shares (BuildSupply, MetalTrade UZ, PolymerGroup, etc.) and analyzes total amounts/costs for essential materials like timber (Yog'och taxta), steel rebar (Temir armatura), bricks, cement, and gravel.

#### 3. HR and Workforce Utilization Analysis (`Uzbekistan_data_Eng_!.jpg`)
*   **Workforce Capacity:** Tracks 50 active employees, evaluating average monthly working hours ($180.86\text{ hours/employee}$) against a total monthly payroll of $167\text{M}$.
*   **Payroll vs. Position Matrix:** Detailed salary allocation breakdown showing that positions like Brigadir and Texnik Nazoratchi consume the largest share of the monthly workforce budget.
*   **Operational Time Tracking:** Evaluates cost/labor changes over time with timeline trendlines mapping monthly salary fluctuations against workforce productivity.

### 📈 Dashboard Tuzilishi va Imkoniyatlari

#### 1. Loyihalar Nazorati va Byudjet Paneli (`Uzbekistan_data_Eng_3.jpg`)
*   **Strategik KPIlar:** 10 ta yirik loyihaning umumiy holati: Byudjetdan chetlanish ($4\text{ mlrd}$), Haqiqiy xarajat ($53\text{ mlrd}$) va Rejalashtirilgan xarajat ($57\text{ mlrd}$).
*   **Taqqoslama tahlil:** Har bir loyiha (Yakkasaroy mehmonxonasi, Chilonzor TJM, Olmazor ko'p qavatli uylari va b.) kesimida *Reja va Haqiqat xarajatlari* balansi.
*   **Muddatlar nazorati:** Loyihaning boshlanish/tugash sanalari, mas'ul muhandislar (responsible experts) va loyiha holatlari ulushi.

#### 2. Loyiha Xarajatlari va Materiallar Tahlili (`Uzbekistan_data_Eng_2.jpg`)
*   **Ta'minot zanjiri tahlili:** $68.70\text{ mlrd}$ umumiy xarajat ichidan faqat materiallar xaridiga yo'naltirilgan $23\text{ mlrd}$ mablag' va 10 xil turdagi qurilish xomashyolari tahlili.
*   **Xarajatlar segmentatsiyasi:** Ish haqi, Transport, Kommunal va Materiallar uchun sarflangan o'rtacha xarajatlar balansi.
*   **Logistika va Yetkazib beruvchilar:** Yetkazib beruvchi korxonalar (BuildSupply, MetalTrade UZ, PolymerGroup va b.) ulushi hamda eng ko'p sotib olingan materiallar (Yog'och taxta, Temir armatura, G'isht, Sement, Qum va b.) miqdori.

#### 3. HR va Ishchi Kuchi Samaradorligi Tahlili (`Uzbekistan_data_Eng_!.jpg`)
*   **Shtat unumdorligi:** 50 ta xodimning oylik o'rtacha ish soati ($180.86\text{ soat}$) va $167\text{ mln}$ so'mlik oylik umumiy maosh fondi tahlili.
*   **Lavozimlar kesimi:** Brigadir, Texnik nazoratchi, Elektrik, Santexnik va G'ishtchilarning jami oylik maosh va jami ish soatlari matritsasi.
*   **Dinamik trend:** Vaqtlar o'tishi bilan oylik maoshlar o'zgarishi va ish soatlari unumdorligi grafigi.
